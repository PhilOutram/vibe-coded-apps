How it works
The Pomodoro Technique
25 minutes of focused work, followed by a 5 minute rest. Repeat to build deep, sustained concentration without burnout.
The four dots below the time track your progress through one full cycle of four Pomodoros.
Controls
Play / Pause — start or pause the current session at any time.
Reset — restart the current session's timer without changing your session count.
Skip — jump straight to the next phase (Focus → Rest, or Rest → Focus).
Living Backgrounds
The background scene reflects your actual time of day — dawn, morning, afternoon, late afternoon, dusk, evening, and night — crossfading gently if the period changes while you work.
The ring and accent colour shift with the time of day — cool blues in the morning, warm ambers at dusk, soft indigos at night.
